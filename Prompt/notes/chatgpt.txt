🚀 Prompt Engineering ko direct seekhne ka simple roadmap

### 1. **Basic Samajh lo** (5 min me)

Prompt engineering ka matlab hota hai — **AI ko aisa instruction dena ki wo tumhari requirement ke hisaab se best output de.**

* Clear aur direct bano.
* Context do (kis purpose ke liye chahiye).
* Format specify karo (list, table, code, essay, etc.).

---

### 2. **Direct Practice Karo**

Tumhe tutorial dekhne ki zarurat nahi. Bas ye steps follow karo:

✅ **Experiment Method**

* Ek hi question ko 3 alag style me poochho.
* Compare karo kaunsa output best aata hai.

Example:

* "Explain Python decorators."
* "Explain Python decorators in simple words with example."
* "Explain Python decorators like I am 10 years old with a short code snippet."

👉 Yehi tumhari **practice hai**.

---

### 3. **Structure Use Karo (SIMPLE Formula)**

Har prompt likhte waqt yaad rakho:

**S – Specific** (clear question)
**I – Instruction** (kya chahiye, list/code/example)
**M – Medium** (short/long/table/bullets)
**P – Persona** (kaise explain kare: teacher, expert, kid, friend)
**L – Limitations** (word limit, step limit, etc.)
**E – Examples** (agar possible ho to sample include karo)

---

### 4. **Daily 10 Min Practice**

Roz sirf 2–3 prompts try karo:

* 1 normal (jaise history, science)
* 1 technical (Python, SQL, etc.)
* 1 creative (story, poem, idea)

---

### 5. **AI se feedback lo**

Tum AI ko bol sakte ho:
👉 *“Mera prompt kaise better ban sakta hai? Rewrite karo.”*
Isse tum automatically improve karte jaoge.


# Rewrite my last prompt to make it more effective.
